Vintage Personal Computers

I started tinkering with old computers about 6 years ago as a hobby. I've always used a PC, but I only upgraded my main computer.
A relative of mine brought me a Hewlett Packard 486 that they were going to throw away, but I kept it, and that's when I decided to put together a machine from that era that I had dreamed of at the time.
Since then, I've had several old computers, but the 486s have remained my favorites.

My 3 favorite 486 PCs (...so far)

  1. HP Vectra line
  2. IBM Ps/1, Ps/2
  3. Compaq Deskpro
